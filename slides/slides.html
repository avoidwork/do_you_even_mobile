What's important?

  • Have an idea? Great, prove interest before spending $$$
  • Short feedback loops (everywhere)
  • Plan to fail, hope to succeed
  • Do not make long term plans, everything can change suddenly

How to get started

  • If you're not a technical person, getting into the code... find a competent technical co-founder!
  • You need a continuous integration pipeline
  • You need to be able to automate testing, & deployment
  • Read the Phoenix Project, it's about devops & is a guide wrapped in a story
  • Spend at least 5x on marketing vs technology

Story time

  • Shipping mobile apps today: (none)
  • What happened? Sh*t changed, start-ups pivot and work gets dropped
  • Reused assets / retooled pipelines / shipped new product in under 2 months (repeatedly)
  • Starting new mobile client at work, already off track before I wrote the first line of code due to politics (for real)

Stating the obvious

  • Without sales, you're running out of runway
  • You need to be quick to validate, test & market your ideas
  • Do not spend months or years developing your technology, it'll be deprecated before you ship
  • Web technology is eating the world, even if you focus on mobile your competitors are testing alternative methods to ship sooner
  • Is web tech the only answer? No, of course not... but you should be aware of it's pros & cons so you can make informed decisions to get to market asap!

What gets in the way?

  • Politics, egos & assholes
  • Being inflexible to change (not everything is a nail for your favorite hammer)
  • Internal disagreement & SABOTAGE!!!
  • Seriously, I've experienced teams actively working against me twice (Jason)
  • Managers who do not manage!

How to succeed

  • Understand the problem you're trying to solve
  • Good API design (REST / Richardson Maturity Model 3)
  • Realizing your API is your product (for real)
  • Not investing too much time in creating the client(s)
  • Test early & often (feedback loop)

Why time matters

Grace Hopper: time as distance

  • Time is money, but it's easier to visualize as distance!
  • 1 ns = 11.8"
  • 1 Î¼s = 985'
  • 1 s = 186,553 mi or 300,228 km (moon is 380,400 km away)
  • 1 week = 112,827,254,400 mi or 181,577,864,905 km (sun is 149,597,870.7 km away)

Short feedback loops

  • Burning a day or week, because you're waiting on someone is costing you a lot of time & money!
  • What's the hidden cost of long feedback loops? Wrong decisions are made; you may not support the story your sales team is telling!
  • I gutted a product under direction of a new PO, only to revert the changes once the sales team got the new build (4 weeks later!)

Suggestions

  • Measure everything
  • Web: Marketing landing page first!
  • API: node, go or elixir
  • Web app: reuse your hybrid code! Not doing hybrid? ruby, phoenix or django!
  • JavaScript Single Page App? Lots of effort for ego, & no one cares!
  • Native? Yes please!
  • Hybrid desktop clients? Yes please!
